Heliophorus-androcles

A simple docker container to calculate checksums.

This project is named after the Heliophorus androcles butterfly.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heliophorus_androcles

A wrapper script around some of the coreutils tools, with JC to provide JSON output.
https://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils/
https://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils/manual/html_node/Summarizing-files.html
