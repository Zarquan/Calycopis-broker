    Two step validation

        DataResource refers the the StorageResource it is stored on by uuid.
        StorageResource refers to the DataResources it stores by uuid.
        VolumeMount refers to DataResource
        There is the potential for loops in the references preventing the schema from loading.
        It depends which ones are processed first, storage, data or volumes.
        Solution is to split the validation into two steps
        * locate() - locate all of the objects, creating placeholdres for them
        * validate() - validate the located objects, including linking their references
